🧠 About the Project:

CyraSense is a modern women’s health platform designed to move beyond basic cycle tracking. Instead of just recording dates, it helps users understand their body patterns, recognize changes, and take informed steps toward better health.
The system combines smart tracking, pattern-based analysis, and guided insights to create a safe, supportive, and easy-to-use experience.

🎯 Problem We Address
Most existing apps:
> Only track dates without explaining patterns
> Provide generic or confusing insights
> Ignore pregnancy awareness and cycle delays
> Do not guide users on when to seek help
This leads to low awareness, confusion, and unnecessary anxiety.

💡 Our Solution
CyraSense provides a complete health intelligence system that:
> Tracks menstrual cycles with predictions
> Aalyzes symptoms and patterns over time
> Detects delays and irregularities safely
> Highlights ovulation and pregnancy awareness
> Suggests doctor consultation when needed
> Educates users through guided content
Keeps engagement high with gamified learning

🧠 How It Works
CyraSense uses:
> Rule-based logic
> Basic statistical analysis
> User data patterns
It evaluates:
> Cycle consistency
> Symptom repetition
> Pain trends
> Delays
Based on this, it generates safe, practical insights without alarming users.
